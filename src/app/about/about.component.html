About Prayansh

Prayansh deals in wide ranges of products like Spices, Herbs & Flour. Apart from this, the brand also embraces the exceptional products like Dehydrated products and Seasonings.

Our products are non-irradiated, unadulterated, contains NO artificial colours and NO MSG. In addition, our ingredients are cleaned without the use of chemicals to ensure purity, consistency, colour and taste and only then it is hygienically packed by skilled & trained staff.
